GENERAL AND SPECIFIC POWER OF ATTORNEY
KNOW ALL PERSONS BY THESE PRESENTS, that **Gotham Innovations Inc.**, a New York
corporation with its principal place of business at 247 Hudson Street, Suite 900, New York, NY
10013 (hereinafter, the Principal), acting pursuant to Section 5-1501 et seq. of the New York
General Obligations Law, hereby appoints **Michael T. Rivers**, residing at 780 Lexington
Avenue, Apt 5B, New York, NY 10065 (hereinafter, the Agent or AttorneyninnFact), as its true
and lawful AttorneyninnFact to act in the Principal’s name, place, and stead as hereinafter set
forth.
1. GRANT OF GENERAL AUTHORITY
The Principal grants to the Agent full power and authority to perform every act and exercise
every
power with respect to the business, property, affairs, and operations of the Principal that the
Principal could do if personally present, including but not limited to: (a) managing bank
accounts;
(b) entering into contracts; (c) hiring and supervising employees; (d) purchasing, leasing, or
otherwise dealing with real and personal property; and (e) filing and paying taxes. All acts done
by
the Agent on behalf of the Principal shall have the same validity as acts of the Principal.
2. SPECIFIC AUTHORITY TO EXECUTE LOAN
Without limiting the foregoing, the Principal specifically authorizes the Agent to negotiate,
execute,
and deliver, on behalf of the Principal, a promissory note, loan agreement, or any related
documents providing for a loan in the principal amount of **One Hundred Thousand U.S. Dollars
($100,000 USD)**, together with any security agreements, guarantees, certificates, or other
instruments required by the lender. The Agent is further empowered to amend, renew,
refinance,
or otherwise modify such loan documents and to take any action necessary to fulfill the
obligations
therein.
3. DURATION AND REVOCATION
This Power of Attorney shall become effective immediately upon execution and shall remain in
full
force and effect until revoked in writing by the Principal or until the termination of the Principal’s
existence as a legal entity, whichever occurs first. Such revocation shall be effective upon
delivery
to the Agent; provided, however, that any bona fide third party may rely on the continuing
authority
of the Agent until actual notice of revocation is received.
4. RELIANCE BY THIRD PARTIES

Any person, including any financial institution or governmental body, may rely upon the
representations of the Agent as to all matters regarding powers granted under this instrument.
No
person who acts in reliance on the authority of the Agent shall incur any liability to the Principal
or
the Principal’s successors.
5. GOVERNING LAW
This Power of Attorney shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the
State of New York without regard to its conflictnofnlaws principles.
6. SEVERABILITY
If any provision of this Power of Attorney is held to be invalid or unenforceable, such invalidity or
unenforceability shall not affect the remaining provisions, which shall remain in full force and
effect.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the Principal has executed this Power of Attorney on this 4th day of
